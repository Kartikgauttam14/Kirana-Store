# Welcome to the Kirana-AI Wiki 🌾🤖

## 🚀 The Vision

The goal of Kirana-AI is not just to provide an app, but to provide an **Intelligence Layer** for micro-retailers. We combine the tactile, trusted nature of local stores with the frictionless speed of modern quick-commerce.

---

### 🎨 Core Theme: Organic Intelligence
We believe technology should feel natural. Our design system merges organic grain silhouettes (representing heritage) with digital pulse handle signals (representing high-tech efficiency).
