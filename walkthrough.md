# Project Walkthrough - Global Branding Refresh

I have successfully updated **Kirana-AI** with a more premium, modern, and high-tech visual identity. The new logo moves away from generic icons and combines the traditional roots of a Kirana store with modern AI intelligence.

## 🚀 The New 'Hybrid Pulse-Grain' Logo

The final logo design is a fusion of two powerful concepts:
1.  **The Grain Silhouette (Organic)**: Represents the traditional Indian grocery (Kirana) heritage.
2.  **The Pulse Handle (AI)**: A tech-blue pulse representing digital connectivity and modern efficiency.
3.  **Digital Pixels**: Integrated cutouts signifying data-driven intelligence.

> [!TIP]
> This logo is implemented as a **100% vector SVG component**, meaning it is lightning-fast to load and stays perfectly sharp at any zoom level.
