## Recommendation
For a mobile-first project like **Kirana-AI**, **Concept 1 (Cyber-Banana)** or **Concept 3 (Geometric K)** offer the best scalability for app icons. However, **Concept 4 (Mascot)** adds a unique level of brand memorability.
